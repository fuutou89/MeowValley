Instructions
========================

This animation can run on top of any floor tile.
You can simply add the floor and then the animation 
correctly on top of that floor tile.

There are available colours for each of the floors, 
but feel free to use them as you want.

I've added some animations on top of a floor tile for 
easier development but feel free to use however you want.


Order of layers
-------------------------
[top]   | Spike Animation
[bottom]| Floor
